How to get started with Dingocoin

1. Pick a Wallet

Pick a Wallet from our Wallet Section
A Wallet is used for holding your Dingocoins directly on your Computer/Smartphone.

2. Get some Dingocoins

There are numerous amounts of ways to get Dingocoin. You can buy them, trade for them, get tipped, "mine" them, and more. They are surprisingly endless!

You can get tipped Dingocoin by participating in our community
If that's not your style, the easiest way to have your first Dingocoin is to get them from a faucet.

A Faucet is a website which gives you a small amount of Dingocoin for free to introduce you to the currency! All you need is your Dingocoin address.

You can also get Dingocoin by "mining" it. Mining is when you use your computer to process Dingocoin transactions by other people, and in return you get Dingocoin.
Mining is intended for advanced users only, as it requires a lot of technical knowledge to do. If you are interested read more here
